What is Health Assessment (HA)?

It is a comprehensive questionnaire that aims to capture specifics of various aspects of your health such as your BMI, physical activity, tobacco status, lifestyle and diet, social and emotional health and your medical history.

Why should I take the Health Assessment?

The answers you provide in the HA helps us gain insight into each aspect of your health. We use this information to identify personalized recommendations for your well-being. You will get 250 points for this activity.

How long will this take?

This would take no more than 15 minutes of your time.
