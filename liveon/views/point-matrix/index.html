Earn Points

How to Earn Points

Earn points for doing the right things! When you take actions that have been proven to have a positive impact on health, you’ll earn points. Then redeem rewards for gift cards or merchandise!

Fitness Program Enrollment

50
Points

When you enroll into the Fitness Program, you'll earn points! For more information about program cost, fitness centers in your area and a list of frequently asked questions click on the Fitness Program link

Take Your Health Assessment

100
Points

Complete your Health Assessment to help you understand your overall health including future health risks.

Complete Milestone Assessment

50
Points

You have the option to earn additional Points by completing a milestone assessment. You can access an assessment through the progress check-in module on the dashboard. Milestone Assessments are available to complete at any time.

Track Your Progress

10
Points

It can be rewarding and beneficial to view your improvements. Tracking your progress can help you stay on track with your wellness goals.

Complete Self-Directed Course

50
Points

Complete any one of the self-directed courses to earn points. You will receive Points quarterly up to your maximum limit.

Fitness Center Use: Visit 1

25
Points

You will earn Life Points for your second visit each week at a Fitness Program network fitness center.

Connect a Fitness Device or App

100
Points

When you connect a fitness device or mobile application on the "Manage Devices and Apps" section of the site, you'll earn Life Points!

Track Progress on the Go

5
Points

You can earn Life Points for using a fitness device or mobile application to track the steps you take throughout the day! First, connect your device or mobile app in the “Manage Devices and Apps” section of the site.
